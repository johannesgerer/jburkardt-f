RANDOM32
Run of SCVT on 32 Random Initial Points

We used N=32 random points on the unit sphere as the start of a calculation with SCVT.

Output files you may copy include:

  • random32_output.txt, the output file.

Initial data files you may copy include:

  • random32_init.xyz, the initial generators.
  • random32_init_del.png, an image of the Delaunay triangulation of the initial generators.
  • random32_init_vor.png, an image of the Voronoi diagram of the initial generators.

Final data files you may copy include:

  • random32_final.xyz, the final generators.
  • random32_final_del.png, an image of the Delaunay triangulation of the final generators.
  • random32_final_vor.png, an image of the Voronoi diagram of the final generators.

Last revised on 26 September 2012.
