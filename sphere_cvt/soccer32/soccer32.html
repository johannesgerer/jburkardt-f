SOCCER32
Run of SCVT on 32 Initial Soccer Points

We used N=32 "soccer points" as the start of a calculation. These are the points at the centers of the 32 faces of a soccer ball.

Output files you may copy include:

  • soccer32.out, the output file.

There are data files associated with the initial set of generator points. Files you may copy include:

  • soccer32_init.xyz, the initial generator positions.
  • soccer32_init_del.png, a PNG image of the Delaunay triangulation;
  • soccer32_init_vor.png, a PNG image of the Voronoi diagram;

There are data files associated with the final set of generator points. Files you may copy include:

  • soccer32_final.xyz, the initial generator positions.
  • soccer32_final_del.png, a PNG image of the Delaunay triangulation;
  • soccer32_final_vor.png, a PNG image of the Voronoi diagram;

You can go up one level to the SCVT page.
