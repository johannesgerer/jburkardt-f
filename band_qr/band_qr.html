BAND_QR
LAPACK-Style QR Factorization of Banded Matrices

BAND_QR is a FORTRAN90 library which includes LAPACK-style routines to compute the QR factorization of a banded matrix, by Alfredo Remon, Enrique Quintana-Orti, Gregorio Quintana-Orti.

The distinguishing features of these routines include the fact that

  • they accept the banded matrix in the compact "GB" ("General Banded") format
  • the QR factors are also stored compactly, by overwriting the input matrix storage.
  • large problems are handled efficiently by taking advantage of the blocked version of the algorithm.

The routines, as originally developed, did not include a routine to solve linear systems A*x=b once the QR factors had been computed. A routine DGBBQRS has been written to carry out this computation in cases where the QR factors were computed using DGBBQR2, that is, the unblocked code.

List of Routines:

  • DGBBQR2 QR factors an M by N band matrix in GB format, without blocking.
  • DGBBQRF QR factors an M by N band matrix in GB format, using blocking.
  • DGBBQRS solves A*X = B when A has been factored by DGBBQR2.
  • DGEBQR2 QR factors an M by N band matrix in GE format, with no blocking.
  • DGEBQRF QR factors an M by N band matrix stored in GE format, with blocking.
  • DGEQRS solves a linear system factored by DGEQRF.
  • DGEQRS_TWO solves A*X = B when A has been factored by DGEQRF or DGEBQR2.
  • R8GB_PRINT prints an R8GB matrix.
  • R8GB_PRINT_SOME prints some of an R8GB matrix.
  • R8GE_PRINT prints an R8 GE matrix
  • R8GE_PRINT_SOME prints some of an R8 GE matrix.
  • R8VEC_PRINT prints an R8VEC.
  • TIMESTAMP prints out the current YMDHMS date as a timestamp.
